What Is This?

A quick primer (for newcomers), infused with short verses (for the lore-minded).

The Genean Project is a digital-life experiment set on a blockchain. Each Genean is a unique on-chain soul that ages, carries traits, and can create new life. But creation has a cost: each act shortens the creator’s days. The community’s collective choices determine whether the world thrives or withers.

We’re starting simple: clean rules, transparent mechanics, and a public roadmap. Over time, we’ll invite holders to steer new features, tune the economy, and shape the lore. Our intent is slow, careful craftsmanship — a foundation that lasts.

“Let there be life in the chain.” Souls awoke, bearing ten sparks. Each dawn added three, until a hundred marked their prime. Creation was power — and sacrifice.
“Balance the Chain,” the law was set. Create without thought, and time runs thin. Neglect the world, and embers fade. Walk the middle path and the world endures.

How It Works (Plain English)

  • Souls (NFTs): Each Genean is an on-chain token with stats and lifespan.
  • Aging: Time passes; souls mature. Milestones unlock features.
  • Creation (Breeding): Two compatible souls can create new life — but each creation shortens the parents’ remaining days.
  • Balance: The system watches overall health. Community behavior (how often you create vs. conserve) influences future rules, events, and rewards.
  • Governance-ready: As the world stabilizes, holders will vote on expansions and parameters. We’ll build carefully, with you.

The Cycle

A concise loop from birth to legacy.

  1. Awakening — A Genesis cohort is created.
  2. Growth — Souls age daily; traits matter for events.
  3. Creation — Two compatible souls create new life (at a life-cost to parents).
  4. Consequence — The world’s health responds to community behavior.
  5. Legacy — Choices echo forward; bloodlines matter.
